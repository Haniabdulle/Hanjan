Our Story

Family run since '17

At Hanjan, our purpose is to bring our community together over a drink - whether it be over one of our locally roasted brews, or a nice cold beer. Pair that with one of our signature Korean dishes, and indulge in our famous desserts, or two.

Our Goals

Our goal is to curate a menu consisting of traditional Korean dishes not commonly found in the local Korean food scene along with well known Korean staples. Korean street food, Korean comfort food and Korean Anju (Food served with liquor) all have a place in our small but focused menu.
